Our Origin

WE RUN
ON
OBSESSION.

2019

Founded in 2019 by two engineers who couldn't find a shoe that matched their pace, STRYD was built in a garage in Bangalore with one clear goal: make the fastest, most comfortable shoe ever engineered in India.

2019Founded
164+Shoe Styles
50K+Athletes
The Mission

Engineering
Forward
Motion.

Every shoe we build starts in the lab — with biomechanical data, athlete feedback, and a relentless drive to do one thing better. We don't release a shoe until it's passed 2,000 hours of real-world testing.

STRYD is an Indian brand built for Indian roads — the heat, the monsoon, the chaos, and the climb. No compromise.

Our Promise

Every STRYD shoe is built with sustainable materials, tested by real athletes, and backed by a lifetime craftsmanship guarantee. If your shoe fails you, we rebuild it — no questions asked.

30%Recycled Materials
